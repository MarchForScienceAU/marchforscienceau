The rally will begin at midday with talks on the lawn of the State Library of Victoria (328 Swanston St, Melbourne)

Auslan interpreters are available. Please contact us (melboune@marchforscienceaustralia.org) if you would like to reserve a place at the front for clear viewing.

At about 1pm, the march will storm steps of parliament, and then end up at the botanical gardens.

At the end of the march, and after much entertainment and relaxation at the gardens we encourage everyone to mingle at the Bull & Bear to mingle with your fellow scientists and science supporters, as well as to connect with individuals and organisations.

Public toilets are available along the way (more details to come).
